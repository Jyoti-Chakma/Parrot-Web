Parrot APP FEATURES

Parrot is a Complete App for Making Quotes & Editing Photos

Decent Photo Editor

Parrot has a Decent Photo Editor which Enables to use all the Features that exists like Applying Different Effects, Adding Text, chose Background etc. You can also Edit your Personal Photos from your Local Drives.

Make Reach Quotes

With the Photo Editor, you can Make your Own Quotes with all Features. You can Chose Existing Quotes for Designing or add New Quotes

Download Background

You can Download Background from more than 18 Cathegories from Pixabay. You can also Chose Colors from the App

Instant Edit

You can open Camera from the App Directly and Instantly Edit the Photo

Save & Share

You can Save & Share after making Quotes or Editing Photos. You can customize weather you want to Save the Images Always or not.

Image Safe Search

Safe Search helps filter out all the Explicit Image from Querries. It's very useful for Children for Safe Use. Not only are all images properly filtered using safe search, the nature of the websites associated with image results are also screened. This prevents users from landing on a site that may contain harmful content, even if the image that brought them to the site is innocent
